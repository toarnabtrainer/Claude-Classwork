# ✨ Productivity Templates to Copy

## 1. Professional Email Template

```text
Rewrite this email professionally.

Audience:
[recipient type]

Goal:
[what the email should achieve]

Tone:
[polite / firm / warm / concise]

Original:
[PASTE EMAIL]

Return:
1. Subject line
2. Improved email
3. Shorter version
```

---

## 2. Meeting Notes Template

```text
Convert these notes into meeting minutes.

Return:
1. Objective
2. Key discussion points
3. Decisions
4. Action items with owner and deadline
5. Risks
6. Follow-up email

Notes:
[PASTE NOTES]
```

---

## 3. Weekly Planning Template

```text
Act as my productivity assistant.

Here are my tasks:
[PASTE TASKS]

Create:
1. Priority ranking
2. Today’s plan
3. Weekly plan
4. Tasks to delay
5. Risks
6. Focus recommendations
```

---

## 4. Business Report Template

```text
Act as a business analyst.

Create a short report from these notes:
[PASTE NOTES]

Include:
1. Executive summary
2. Situation
3. Key findings
4. Risks
5. Recommendations
6. Next steps
```

---

## 5. Decision Matrix Template

```text
Help me decide between these options:
[LIST OPTIONS]

Compare them based on:
1. Cost
2. Time
3. Risk
4. Benefit
5. Ease of execution

Return a table and final recommendation.
```
