# EcoServe Project Knowledge Base

## 1. Company Overview

EcoServe is a fictional sustainability operations platform for small and medium businesses.

EcoServe helps customers:

```text
- Submit waste pickup requests
- Track pickup status
- Record recycling activity
- Generate monthly sustainability summaries
```

EcoServe does not provide:

```text
- Legal compliance advice
- Tax advice
- Environmental certification guarantees
- Vendor performance guarantees
```

---

## 2. EcoServe Plans

| Plan | Intended Customer | Main Features |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | Small office | Waste pickup requests, recycling log, monthly email summary |
| Team | Multi-location business | Location dashboard, user roles, pickup status, monthly summary |
| Pro | Operations-heavy business | Advanced reporting, exportable data, priority support |

Pricing is not included in this knowledge base.

---

## 3. Support Hours

Standard support hours are:

```text
Monday to Friday
9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local business time
```

---

## 4. Pickup Request Process

Customers submit a pickup request by:

```text
1. Opening the Pickup tab
2. Selecting the location
3. Choosing the waste category
4. Adding a preferred pickup window
5. Submitting the request
```

---

## 5. Pickup Status

Pickup status normally updates when the partner vendor:

```text
- Confirms the pickup
- Starts the pickup
- Completes the pickup
- Cancels the pickup
```

---

## 6. Missing Pickup Status Process

If pickup status is missing, support should request:

```text
- Customer account name
- Location
- Pickup request ID
- Preferred callback email
```

Then the case should be routed to:

```text
Vendor Operations
```

---

## 7. Vendor Dispute Process

If a customer complains that a vendor missed a pickup, support should:

```text
- Acknowledge the concern
- Avoid blaming the customer or vendor
- Avoid promising vendor action unless confirmed
- Route the issue to Vendor Operations
```

---

## 8. Privacy Requests

If a customer asks to delete or access personal data, support should route the request to:

```text
Privacy Desk
```

Support should not provide legal advice.

---

## 9. Response Tone Guide

Support responses should be:

```text
- Helpful
- Calm
- Professional
- Customer-friendly
- Concise
```

Support responses should avoid:

```text
- Blame language
- Unsupported promises
- Legal guarantees
- Certification guarantees
- Vendor commitments not confirmed
```

---

A customer FAQ Artifact should include:


```text

- Legal compliance advice
```
