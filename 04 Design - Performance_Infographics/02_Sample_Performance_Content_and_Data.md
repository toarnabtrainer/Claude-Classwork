## 1. Company background

NorthBridge Business Services Ltd. is a fictional B2B services company operating across three regions. It provides managed services and digital solutions to mid-sized and enterprise customers.

The company’s management team is reviewing Q2 2026 performance to decide which issues require immediate action during the next quarter.

---

## 2. Executive performance message

> NorthBridge continued to grow in Q2 2026, but revenue remained below target and profitability weakened because of margin pressure and slower cost improvement. Customer advocacy improved, while retention and delivery reliability declined. Management should prioritise margin recovery, account retention, and operating discipline.

## 11. Confirmed performance drivers

The following drivers are supported by the fictional case evidence:

- Digital Solutions revenue was $1.1m below target.
- Digital Solutions had a lower gross margin than Managed Services.
- Project mix and implementation overruns contributed to margin pressure.
- Cost-efficiency benefits were realised more slowly than planned.
- Service-level performance and delivery reliability declined.
- Seven high-value accounts require active recovery plans.
- North was the only region to exceed its revenue target.

---

## 12. Management hypotheses requiring validation

The following statements should be labelled as hypotheses rather than confirmed facts:

- workload pressure may be contributing to service-quality deterioration;
- pricing discipline may differ by region and customer segment;
- project-estimation quality may be contributing to Digital Solutions overruns;
- slower support resolution may be contributing to retention decline;
- regional channel capability may explain part of the West revenue shortfall.

---

## 13. Recommended management actions

### Priority 1 — Restore margin discipline

- review Digital Solutions pricing, project mix, estimation, and delivery overruns;
- validate the delayed savings in the Cost Efficiency Programme;
- establish a gross-margin recovery target for Q3 2026.

**Success measures:** gross margin, project overrun rate, realised savings, contribution margin by business unit.

### Priority 2 — Protect high-value customer retention

- assign executive sponsors to the seven accounts requiring recovery plans;
- define service, commercial, and relationship actions for each account;
- review progress weekly until risk reduces.

**Success measures:** retention, at-risk revenue, support service level, account health.

### Priority 3 — Improve delivery discipline

- focus on on-time delivery, first-time-right performance, and process-cycle time;
- analyse rework and bottlenecks by region and business unit;
- separate sustainable productivity gains from excessive workload.

**Success measures:** on-time delivery, first-time-right rate, cycle time, utilisation, employee workload indicators.
