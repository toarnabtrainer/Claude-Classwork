# 🔶 Module 2 Practice Resource — GreenOffice Business Scenario

## Scenario

GreenOffice Supplies is a fictional company selling eco-friendly office products to small businesses.

## Situation

The company is planning a new B2B sales campaign. The team has the following notes:

```text
- Target customers: small offices with 20–100 employees
- Products: recycled paper, eco pens, refillable markers, desk organizers
- Main benefit: reduce office waste
- Campaign duration: 30 days
- Channels: email, LinkedIn, phone follow-up
- Concern: pricing is slightly higher than standard office supplies
- Opportunity: many companies want sustainability reporting
```

## Practice Tasks

Learners should use Claude to create:

```text
1. Professional outreach email
2. 30-day campaign plan
3. Sales objection response
4. Weekly task plan
5. Short executive report
```

## Important Constraint

```text
❌ Do not claim certified environmental impact unless provided.
✅ Use careful wording such as "designed to support lower-waste purchasing."
```
